About the movie

Inception is a 2010 science fiction action film about a thief, Dom Cobb, who extracts information from people's dreams. He is hired to do the opposite: plant an idea into a target's mind, known as "inception". Cobb assembles a team to infiltrate multiple layers of dreams to achieve this seemingly impossible task. The film explores themes of reality, loss, and the power of the subconscious
